How I play basketball

I have been playing this beautiful game since I was 6 years of age, I started playing the game with my cousins at my grandmother's house, there's an empty space inside the house which is a bit far from the main building, so my cousins installed a hoop there and we play the sport there, it is always amazing to play with them. That was how I started, I still play the game at my school when I have the energy and time to, and back at home, my friend has a hoop in their front yard and a couple people usually come and play. My normal position is a small forward because of my passing ability and court vision, but whenever there are a lot bigger players than me(like about 6'5+), I prefer to play the point guard position, because I am not considered as tall in the game even with a height of 6'0,

The game is really beautiful, it increases the body's fitness and so much more, I also like the game because it is more technical than a lot of sports out there, and you can literally see the physics in the game wheather watching or playing.
