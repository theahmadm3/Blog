Astronomy

Astronomy is the study of everything in the universe beyond Earth's atmosphere. That includes objects we can see with our naked eyes, like the Sun , the Moon , the planets, and the stars . It also includes objects we can only see with telescopes or other instruments, like faraway galaxies and tiny particles.

This is one of my favorite topics in science as it helps me understand the universe in a more advanced way, I have loved this topic from a very young


Quantum Physics

Quantum physics is the study of the behavior of matter and energy at the molecular, atomic, nuclear, and even smaller microscopic levels. In the early 20th century, scientists discovered that the laws governing macroscopic objects do not function the same in such small realms

I also love this part of science as it is what further explains the abstraction that astronomy gives us about the universe. It broadens the math behind all the theories in astronomy and it also gives basis to astronomy. I study this part of science on my own, it is interesting especially for a curious person like me.
